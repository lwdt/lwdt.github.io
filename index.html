Why Publish a Blog?

July 9, 2021hyperlink 🔗

I’ve been dwelling on this question for a while now. There is some part of me, somewhere I can’t place, that keeps pulling me back to the blog. I’m not sure if it’s envy of others’ successes, vanity that demands attention, or a humanistic instinct to “let knowledge grow from more to more; and so be human life enriched,” as my alma mater proclaimed.

Perhaps this blog is the product of boredom or a confrontation with the existential dread of invisibility. Regardless, here we are.
